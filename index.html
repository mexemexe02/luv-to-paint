A Passion for Transforming Spaces

Based right here in Barrie, Ontario, Luv To Paint was built on a simple belief: every home deserves to feel beautiful. We're not just painters — we're craftspeople who genuinely care about the spaces you live in.

Whether it's refreshing a single room or reimagining your entire home, we bring the same dedication to every project. No shortcuts, no rushed jobs — just quality work from people who love what they do.

  • Locally owned & operated in Barrie, Ontario
